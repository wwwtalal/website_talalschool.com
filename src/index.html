About Our School

Talal International School has been a beacon of educational excellence in Jeddah for over two decades, nurturing young minds with separated schools for boys and girls.

Excellence in Education

Two decades of proven academic excellence with a commitment to providing high-quality international education that prepares students for global success.

Separated Learning Environment

Dedicated boys and girls schools ensuring focused learning environments that respect cultural values while maintaining international standards.

International Standards

Following world-renowned British curriculum to provide students with globally recognized qualifications and comprehensive education.

Our Foundation

Talal International School, Jeddah opened its doors with a vision to provide exceptional education in a nurturing environment. Today, the school proudly educates a diverse student body representing a wide range of nationalities.

Our Identity

TIS is a vibrant, gender-segregated international school in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia offering a curriculum designed to empower learners for lifelong success. TIS is recognized by the Saudi Ministry of Education and adheres to global educational standards.

Our Educators

Our team of educators includes certified teachers and qualified professionals from from diverse countries, creating a rich blend of expertise and cultural understanding.

Our Mission

The school emphasizes academic excellence through engaging, student-centered classrooms that foster confidence and creativity. In line with our mission to guide, groom, and motivate students beyond expectations, TIS is continuously evolving to incorporate best practices and internationally benchmarked programs that shape responsible global citizens.

Our Curriculum

We follow the internationally recognized British curriculum designed to provide comprehensive education from KG-1 to Grade 9.

British Curriculum

Following the prestigious British educational system, preparing students for international qualifications and global opportunities with a focus on holistic development.

  • Creative and critical thinking focus
